# KarghaKendra - Phase Plan & Future Roadmap

## Platform Vision & Social Impact
KarghaKendra is dedicated to organizing the unorganized market of textiles by connecting grassroots fabric makers and laborers directly to large-scale enterprises. By facilitating bulk orders and expanding their customer base, the platform empowers weavers economically and socially, eliminating exploitative middlemen.

### Core Social Objectives
- **Women's Empowerment:** Providing independent digital identities, direct bank transfers, and flexible work tracking for female artisans to foster financial independence.
- **Unemployment Alleviation:** Creating a centralized task assignment ecosystem that connects an idle workforce with active corporate bulk orders, effectively abolishing localized unemployment.
- **Skill Development:** Integrating government training programs and scheme management directly into the weaver's intuitive dashboard to upskill the workforce.
- **Heritage Preservation:** Digitally cataloging and promoting indigenous handloom techniques, ensuring traditional Indian textile artistry survives and thrives in the modern global market.
- **Financial Inclusion & Fair Trade:** Eliminating predatory middlemen to ensure ethical pricing, while helping weavers build a verifiable digital income history to access micro-loans and institutional credit.

# Phase 1: Foundation & Cooperative ERP

## Objective
Digitize the daily operations of textile cooperatives, establish transparent member management, and streamline basic business flows.

## Features
### Cooperative ERP & Finance
- Digital Payments & Payment Tracking
- Revenue and Expense Dashboard
- Invoice Generation
- Offline Support & Low Bandwidth Mode

### Member Management
- Add, Edit, and Remove Members
- Assign Skills and Track Productivity
- Role-Based Authentication (Aadhaar & DigiLocker Verification)

### Orders & Inventory
- Create, Assign, and Track Orders
- Material and Batch Tracking
- Low Stock Alerts

### Procurement Management
- Purchase Requests and Purchase Orders
- Supplier Directory Management

## Social Impact Focus
Formalizing the unorganized sector by providing workers with verified digital identities and ensuring transparent, direct financial transactions to prevent wage exploitation.

# Phase 2: Market Access & Production Tracking

## Objective
Connect cooperatives directly with enterprise buyers and implement rigorous tracking to ensure timely, high-quality delivery.

## Features
### Buyer Portal & Marketplace
- Product and Cooperative Listings
- Browse, Search, and Filter Products
- Request Quotations and Place Bulk Orders
- Direct Buyer-Cooperative Chat and Price Negotiation

### Production Tracking
- Production Workflow Tracking (Warping, Dyeing, Weaving, Finishing)
- Live Progress Updates and Worker Assignment
- Delay Detection and Completion Tracking

### Quality Management
- Stage-wise Production Updates
- Upload Production Images for Buyer Verification (via Cloudinary)
- Standardized Issue Reporting

## Social Impact Focus
Providing direct market access to boost employment by unlocking larger corporate orders, bypassing intermediaries, and retaining higher profit margins for the cooperatives.

# Phase 3: Intelligence & AI Integration

## Objective
Leverage Python-based AI services to optimize production, predict market trends, and provide accessible assistance to less tech-savvy users.

## Features
### AI Production Planning
- Smart Workforce Allocation
- Material Requirement Forecasting
- Delivery Timeline Estimation

### Demand Forecasting
- Seasonal and Festival Demand Prediction
- Market Trend Analysis

### Smart Procurement & Analytics
- Automated Quotation Comparison
- Production, Employment, and Financial Analytics Reports

### AI Assistant & Accessibility
- Voice Commands and Read Aloud features
- Multilingual Support for regional weavers
- Production and Inventory Query Resolution

## Social Impact Focus
Bridging the digital divide. Voice-activated, multilingual AI ensures that illiterate or older artisans—especially women in rural areas—can navigate the platform seamlessly and maximize their earning potential.

# Phase 4: Government Integration & Global Scaling

## Objective
Align with state and national frameworks to distribute benefits and scale the platform to an international export ecosystem.

## Features
### Government Integration
- Government Dashboard (Production & Employment Statistics)
- Cooperative Monitoring and Document Verification
- District and State-level Reporting

### Scheme Management
- Publish Grants and Financial Aids
- Rollout Government Training Programs
- Direct Scheme Application and Disbursement Tracking

### National Cooperative Network
- Inter-cooperative Communication (Broadcasts, Group Chat)
- Shared Resource and Procurement Pools across regions

### Export Ecosystem
- Global Marketplace Localization
- Multi-currency and Export Compliance handling
- International Shipping and Delivery Tracking

## Social Impact Focus
Driving nationwide unemployment-abolishing schemes by providing the government with real-time grassroots data. Facilitating global export capabilities to elevate indigenous crafts and secure long-term economic stability for weaving communities.
